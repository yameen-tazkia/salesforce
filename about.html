The thinking behind our name

Two ideas, one practice

Our name is a deliberate signal of how we approach enterprise transformation. It pairs a centuries-old idea of purification and growth with a modern reading of intelligence as a discipline.

Tazkia تزكية

Purification and purposeful growth

Tazkia is an Arabic word, rooted in the Qur'an, that carries two interconnected meanings. The careful cleansing of intentions, actions, and processes from what holds them back. And the patient nurturing of what is good, so it can grow and reach its full potential.

Applied to enterprise, it means more than removing inefficiency or complexity. It means helping organisations grow sustainably, intelligently, and with clear purpose.

Intelligence

Three dimensions, one practice

Intelligence, to us, lives in three places at once.

  • Artificial Intelligence. Deep expertise in Salesforce, Agentforce, agentic AI, and enterprise automation.
  • Business Intelligence. The strategic insight, analytics, and reporting that turn data into decisions.
  • Human Intelligence. The ethical judgement, empathy, and integrity that make innovation responsible.
