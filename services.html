Services

AI-native delivery, end-to-end on Salesforce

From agentic AI strategy to Agentforce engineering, Slack workflows, and adoption — a portfolio designed for enterprises that want AI working alongside their people, not in a separate stack.

01 / Strategy

Salesforce AI Strategy & Readiness Assessment

A structured assessment of your Salesforce footprint, data foundation, and operational readiness — paired with a prioritized portfolio of agentic AI use cases and a roadmap your leadership can stand behind.

  • Current-state Salesforce architecture and Data Cloud readiness review
  • AI use-case discovery, scoring, and value sizing
  • Target operating model and capability gap analysis
  • Phased 6 / 12 / 24 month roadmap with investment view
  • Executive briefing pack and steering committee onboarding
02 / Build

Agentforce & Agentic AI Implementation

Design, build, and operationalize AI agents on Salesforce — service agents, autonomous sales assistants, and back-office co-pilots — engineered to collaborate with your teams, not work in isolation.

  • Agent design: persona, scope, tools, and guardrails
  • Knowledge grounding with Data Cloud and Knowledge Articles
  • Tool orchestration via Flow, Apex, and external services
  • Evaluation harness, regression testing, and quality gates
  • Production hardening, observability, and SLOs
03 / Collaborate

Slack Integration & Agentforce in Slack

Bring Salesforce records, Agentforce agents, and approval workflows into Slack — so humans and AI collaborate in the flow of work, with answers, decisions, and customer context one channel away.

  • Salesforce-Slack integration: record actions, alerts, and channel routing
  • Agentforce agents accessible in Slack DMs and channels
  • Slack Workflow Builder automations for sales, service, and ops
  • Account, deal, and customer channels with Slack Connect
  • Knowledge capture, AI search, and intelligent summarization

Why it matters for the business

  • Faster decisions — context, data, and AI surfaced in the channel, not buried in tabs.
  • Higher productivity — fewer app switches; routine work handled by agents and workflows.
  • Stronger collaboration — customers, partners, and internal teams aligned in shared channels.
  • Compounding knowledge — conversations become a searchable, AI-enhanced organizational memory.
04 / Automate

AI & Intelligent Automation

Re-engineer customer and operational processes with AI, Flow, Data Cloud, and prompt-driven workflows — compressing cycle times, removing friction, and lifting decision quality at scale.

  • Process discovery and high-leverage automation candidates
  • Einstein, Prompt Builder, and Model Builder integration
  • Document understanding and unstructured data activation
  • Human-in-the-loop and exception handling design
  • Continuous improvement instrumentation
05 / Adopt

AI Change, Adoption & Governance

AI literacy, governance, and adoption frameworks that move AI from pilot curiosity to enterprise capability — equipping the humans working alongside agents to do so confidently, productively, and responsibly.

  • AI Council operating model and decision rights
  • Acceptable use, model, and data policies
  • Role-based AI literacy and enablement curricula
  • Adoption analytics and value realization tracking
  • Risk register, audit trails, and red-team exercises
06 / Prove

Agentic Pilot & Proof-of-Value Programs

Time-boxed agentic pilots that validate ROI in a single quarter — proving where AI agents and your teams collaborate best, and building the executive momentum required to scale.

  • Hypothesis-driven scoping with clear success metrics
  • Rapid build sprints on a contained Salesforce footprint
  • Live measurement against baseline KPIs
  • Executive readout with scale-up business case
  • Optional handoff to in-house teams with enablement
