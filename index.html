Agentforce for Service

Global data and analytics company

Design, discovery, requirements gathering, and solution architecture support.

Contributed to the design of a Salesforce Agentforce chatbot deployed across four country websites (UK, Germany, Austria, Switzerland) and four languages. The solution covers three integrated scenarios:

  • Knowledge deflection. Salesforce Knowledge grounded via Data Cloud and the Agentforce Data Library, enabling retrieval-augmented self-service for routine customer queries.
  • Case escalation. Intelligent case creation with contact matching, duplicate handling, and assignment-rule routing into Service Cloud queues.
  • Lead generation. Commercial-intent detection and consented lead capture into Sales Cloud with downstream routing.

Work spanned requirements gathering, success-criteria definition, conversation-flow design, integration with existing Service Cloud automations and validation rules, and a post-interaction CSAT capture model. Delivered as a partner contribution alongside the client's prime implementation team.
