Outcomes from Salesforce + AI engagements

Representative scenarios drawn from common enterprise patterns. Names and figures are illustrative, but the architectures and outcomes mirror real-world programs.

Banking & Financial Services

AI-assisted relationship management for a retail bank

Illustrative case
32%RM productivity
2xLead conversion

Problem

Relationship managers were spending most of their time pulling data, drafting follow-ups, and preparing pitches across siloed systems. Pipeline quality and conversion suffered.

Solution

Designed and deployed an Agentforce-powered relationship co-pilot grounded in Data Cloud — surfacing next-best actions, drafting personalized outreach, and automating CRM hygiene. Brought into Slack via account channels so RMs and product specialists could collaborate on opportunities in real time.

Outcome

RM administrative time reduced by ~32%, qualified pipeline doubled, and CSAT improved across high-value segments. Governance council now manages a portfolio of agents in production.

Real Estate & Property

Intelligent lead qualification for a regional developer

Illustrative case
45%Faster response
+18%Booked viewings

Problem

High inbound volume across web, WhatsApp, and portals overwhelmed the inside-sales team. Hot leads cooled before consultants could respond, eroding conversion.

Solution

Built an AI qualification agent on Service Cloud and Agentforce — triaging conversations in Arabic and English, asking discovery questions, and routing qualified leads with rich context to the right consultant.

Outcome

First-response time fell 45%, booked viewings rose 18%, and consultants spent more time with high-intent buyers. Marketing now optimizes spend against agent-validated lead quality.

Retail & E-commerce

Service automation for a multi-brand retail group

Illustrative case
60%Cases auto-resolved
-28%Cost to serve

Problem

Service teams across multiple brands were drowning in repetitive cases — order status, returns, and product questions — while VIP customer experience suffered from delayed responses.

Solution

Implemented a Service Cloud + Agentforce stack with grounded knowledge, order-system integrations, and clear escalation paths. Slack swarming channels routed complex cases to specialists and brought leadership real-time visibility. Built a governance model and evaluation harness to safely expand agent scope over time.

Outcome

Around 60% of incoming cases resolved without agent involvement, cost-to-serve down 28%, and human agents redeployed to high-value VIP and complex resolution work.
